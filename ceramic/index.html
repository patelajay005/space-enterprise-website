About Us

Pioneering Ceramic Tile Raw Material Solutions

Founded in 2012, Space Enterprise has grown into a trusted name in the ceramic tile industry supplying premium raw materials and consumables to tile manufacturers across India and beyond.

We specialize in abrasives, diamond tools and nano finishing products engineered for GVT (Glazed Vitrified Tiles), PGVT (Polished Glazed Vitrified Tiles) and full-body vitrified tile production lines. Our products are designed to deliver consistent grinding performance, precise edge finishing and mirror-like surface quality.

With deep industry expertise and a commitment to quality, we partner with tile manufacturers to optimize their production processes — reducing downtime, improving yield, and achieving superior finish quality.
