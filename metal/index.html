About Metal Scrap Division

Reliable Metal Scrap Trading & Supply

Alongside our ceramic raw materials business, Space Enterprise supplies and procures ferrous and non-ferrous metal scrap for recyclers, foundries, smelters and industrial buyers in India and across international markets.

We source clean, segregated metal scrap with consistent grades from aluminium UBC and alloy wheels to brass, copper and ingots. Our team ensures proper sorting, weighing and documentation for smooth cross-border transactions.

Whether you need bulk non-ferrous scrap for remelting or ferrous scrap for steel production, we provide competitive pricing, end-to-end logistics coordination and transparent dealings worldwide.
